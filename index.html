Gemslingers, like yourself, are searching for unique gems scattered across the wild west. Discover these gems to add to your collection, but watch out because other Gemslingers want what you have!

Producers

Coen has been designing board and card games since before he can remember. Usually only on flippity-floppity paper, though. One day, he had a crazy idea for a fast-pace, chaos-riddled, card-slinging free-for-all. Everyone who played it agreed it should become a real live thing: no more flitty paper bits slip-sliding around the table tops! His friend Nathan kindly agreed to take on the challenge of creating the rock-steady theme for the game, as well as the digital art that would grace the new sturdier deck. And thus was the above colorful box of joy made real in this world, yeehaw!
